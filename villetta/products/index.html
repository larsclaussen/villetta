LIMONCELLO

Limoncello is the traditional liqueur of the Amalfi Coast in Southern Italy. A smooth, sweet & sour lemon liqueur with only organic ingredients and without any artificial coloring, flavoring or conservatives. Fresh & heartwarming.

buy

LEMONS FROM SORRENTO

Distilled from the peel of Sorrento lemons, exceptionally thick and rich in essential oils giving our Limoncello its unique aroma, taste and color. A lemon of superior quality and unlike any other, with protected geographical status in the EU.

visit

HANDPICKED & HANDPEELED

We select our finest lemons and carefully pick and peel them by hand. To ensure a smooth taste, we only put the yellow zest of the peel, not the bitter white, in organic alcohol to extract the essential oils. Then we filter the infusion.

buy

AMSTERDAM DUNE WATER

We mix the lemon zest infusion with organic sugar water. Our water is naturally filtered by the Amsterdam dunes and is among the cleanest drinking water in the world. And pure water makes pure Limoncello.

visit

SMALL BATCHES

Our Limoncello is distilled in small batches to ensure exceptional quality. For each distillation we take our time. So we can produce sustainable cellos that are delicious, eco friendly and fair. In the Italian slow food tradition.

ARANCELLO

Arancello is the traditional liqueur of Sicily in Southern Italy. A smooth, bittersweet orange liqueur with only organic ingredients and without any artificial coloring, flavoring or conservatives. Tangy & heartwarming. Pure, on the rocks, or mixed up, drink your cellos any way you like.

BLOOD ORANGES FROM SICILY

Distilled from the peel of Sicilian blood oranges, gorgeous red and raspberry-like giving our Arancello its unique aroma, taste and color. An orange that only grows on the exceptional fertile volcanic soil around Mount Etna.
