COCKTAILS

    LIMONCELLO ESPRESSO

    Ingredients

  • 30 ml Limoncello
  • 30 ml Espresso, cold
  • 30 ml Baileys

Recipe

Fill a cocktail glass with 2 ice cubes. Add 30ml Limoncello, 30ml Espresso and 30ml Baileys and mix the ingredients. Garnish with raspberries on a cocktail stick.

COCKTAILS

    MOJITO LIMONCELLO

    Ingredients

  • 60 ml Limoncello
  • 45 ml Sparkling water
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 30 ml lemon juice
  • 6 sprigs of mint
  • Crushed ice

Recipe

Lightly bruise the sprigs of mint with the back of a spoon in a long drink glass. Pour the 60 ml Limoncello, 30 ml lemon juice and 2 tablespoons of sugar into the glass. Fill the glass with crushed ice. Top it off with sparkling water.

COCKTAILS

    LIMONCELLO SGROPPINO

    Ingredients

  • 30 ml Limoncello
  • 60 ml Prosecco
  • Lemon sorbet ice-cream, two scoops
  • slice of lemon

Recipe

Put two scoops of ice-cream into a blender. Add 60ml Prosecco and 30ml Limoncello and mix the ingredients. Serve the Sgroppino in a champagne glass, garnished with a slice of lemon.

COCKTAILS

    TOM COLLINS LIMONCELLO

    Ingredients

  • 30 ml Limoncello
  • 45 ml Gin
  • 15 ml lemon juice
  • 2/3 ice cubes
  • Sparkling water
  • Slice of lemon

Recipe

Fill a cocktail shaker with 30 ml Limoncello, 45 ml Gin and 15 ml lemon juice and shake the ingredients. Pour the mix into a long drink glass filled with ice cubes and a slice of lemon. Top it off with some sparkling water.

COCKTAILS

    LIMONCELLO & TONIC

    Ingredients

  • 30 ml Limoncello
  • 120 ml Tonic
  • Fresh basil
  • Ice cubes

Recipe

Put 3/4 ice cubes in a big wine glass. Pour 30ml Limoncello and 120ml Tonic into the glass. Add 2/3 basil leaves, lightly bruised.

COCKTAILS

    LIMONCELLO POPSICLE

    Ingredients

  • 30 ml Limoncello
  • 100 ml water
  • 20 ml lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • grated lemon zest

Recipe

Put all the ingredients into a large measuring cup and mix them thoroughly. Pour the mixture into popsicle molds and freeze for at least three hours.
